03The 10-pillar Certification — where a beverage programme is scored

Every IX-certified operator is scored across ten pillars. The four most beverage-relevant:

PillarWhat's evidenced
02 · SustainabilitySourcing standards (chemical-free / Red Tractor / MSC / ASC / Fairtrade where applicable), waste diversion, packaging, plastic-bottle policy. Drinks programme decisions sit here.
05 · Customer ExperienceService standards, menu accuracy (allergen, pricing, descriptions), seasonal menu cadence, accessibility, transparent pricing. Beverage list rigour is part of this score.
06 · Supply ChainApproved-supplier list, due diligence, fair payment terms (max 30 days), local preference, audit cadence. The supplier-recommendation lens.
10 · Economic ParticipationProfit-sharing, staff equity, community investment. Affects how a venue's brand-side trade education programme is structured.

Scoring is evidence-based and decays over time without re-evidencing — a consultant's work has visible, ongoing impact on the venue's IX rating, not just a one-off audit number.
