Stage 0 — Founding Preferential Return (the bit that's most novel)
Mechanics: Founding investors in any venue-specific raise (Direct Business NFT, Development Convertibles for that venue, venue-specific Participation Notes) receive 100% of that venue's distributable cashflow until they have received (a) 6% per annum cumulative preferred return on subscription price, and (b) full repayment of principal. 0% carry to IX during Stage 0.

Ringfenced. Stage 0 is per-venue only. It does not apply to pooled vehicles (IXF Units, pooled Cashflow Notes), which are pari passu under Stage 1. So Founding investors in venue A do not get preference from venue B's cashflows; pooled-vehicle LPs are not diluted by per-venue Founding tranches in other venues. The corporate-level Articles of Association v2.1 codify this as Article 34, Stage 0.

Why Stage 0 instead of generic 7% pref + catch-up: The generic PE waterfall structure muddles per-venue performance with fund-level performance. Stage 0 isolates the Founding Investor preferential to the venue's own cashflow, which makes the carry math defensible per-venue and prevents cross-subsidy.
What replaces what. The earlier IX score-×-multiple-×-revenue formula is deprecated. v2.0 is stage-dependent DCF + capex floor + explicit debt deduction + lease renewal probability. References standard UK hospitality M&A practice. Quarterly reassessment cadence. Where this would fail FTI-grade DD — that's the question I'm sending you.

04NAV roll-up & reporting

Fund NAV rolls up from the aggregate Business NFT valuations + the loan books (Cashflow Notes + Development Convertibles + Participation Notes + PropCo Notes). Quarterly published. Each Business NFT carries a stage (R1–R5), a certification tier (F/V/E), and a governance rating (G1–G5) — all of which feed into its WACC and therefore its DCF.

Per the Articles v2.1 + Reg Compliance Framework v2.1: investor-facing disclosures include quarterly NAV statements, Founding Preferential Return satisfaction status per venue, capex evidence file status per venue, and stage transitions (R5→R1) with IC sign-off audit trail. Independent valuer engagement is on the legal track (template drafted, not yet engaged).
