Privacy Policy

Initiative X Ltd — Last updated 16 April 2026

Who we are

Initiative X Ltd ("IX", "we", "us") operates the IX membership platform, connecting members with a network of premium hospitality, wine, travel, and lifestyle businesses. Our registered address is 8 Slingsby Place, London WC2E 9AB, United Kingdom.

For data protection queries, contact us at hello@initiativex.co.uk.

What data we collect

We collect the following personal data when you join IX or use our services:

  • Identity: first name, last name
  • Contact: email address, phone number
  • Membership: tier (Explorer/Patron), member ID, join date, subscription status
  • Preferences: dining style, dietary requirements, occasion types, lifestyle interests, vertical affiliations
  • Activity: visit history, booking records, rewards balance, interaction history with our concierge
  • Payment: processed securely by Stripe. We do not store card numbers.
  • Technical: device type and browser when using our services (no tracking cookies)

Why we collect it

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • Personalisation: tailoring recommendations, offers, and concierge responses to your preferences
  • Service delivery: managing your membership, processing bookings, issuing wallet passes, tracking rewards
  • Communication: booking confirmations, reminders, and (if you opted in) newsletters and event invitations
  • Network coordination: sharing relevant preferences with partner venues so they can prepare for your visit (e.g. dietary needs, seating preference)
  • Improvement: understanding how members use IX to improve the platform

Legal basis: consent (provided at sign-up), legitimate interest (service delivery), and contract performance (membership terms).

Who we share it with

We share your data only with:

  • IX partner venues (e.g. UKIYO) — only the preferences relevant to your visit (name, dietary needs, tier). Never your full profile.
  • Stripe — payment processing for Patron subscriptions
  • Airtable — secure member database hosting (EU/UK data centres)
  • Google — wallet pass generation (Google Wallet API)
  • Email provider — transactional emails (booking confirmations, wallet pass delivery)

We never sell your data. We never share it with advertisers.

How long we keep it

  • Active members: for the duration of your membership
  • After you leave: 24 months, then permanently deleted
  • Financial records: 7 years (UK legal requirement)
  • Marketing consent records: retained as evidence of consent until withdrawn

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your data — request a copy of everything we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Port your data to another service
  • Withdraw consent for marketing at any time
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Complain to the ICO (ico.org.uk) if you believe we've mishandled your data

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@initiativex.co.uk. We'll respond within 30 days.

Cookies

Our website does not use tracking cookies. We do not use analytics that identify individual users. If this changes, we will update this policy and ask for your consent.

Children

IX is not intended for anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top will change. For significant changes, we'll notify you by email.
