02Stage 0 founding preferential return — what it looks like in practice

Active position · UKIYO venue-specific raise
Sample investor holds £10K in the UKIYO Direct Business NFT founding tranche

The Stage 0 Founding Preferential Return is the per-venue ringfenced 6% p.a. cumulative preference. For this position:

  • Distributable cashflow source: UKIYO OpCo only — not pooled with other venues
  • Stage 0 entitlement: 100% of UKIYO's distributable cashflow flows to founding investors until 6% p.a. cumulative + principal repaid
  • Position in the per-venue waterfall: Stage 0 satisfied first; only then does Stage 1 (return of capital pari passu) → Stage 2 (10% IX carry) → Stage 3 (20%) → Stage 4 (25% above 2× MOIC) trigger
  • Cross-subsidy protection: founding investors in UKIYO receive no preferential return from other venues' cashflow, and pooled-vehicle LPs (IXF Units holders) are not diluted by UKIYO's per-venue founding tranche

Per Articles of Association v2.1 Article 34. Visible in the investor dashboard as a per-venue line item with current preferential return accrual against the 6% p.a. cumulative target.
