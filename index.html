The Problem

Independent hospitality is broken

No shared infrastructure

Independent venues build everything from scratch. Every system, every supplier relationship, every process — reinvented each time.

Staff have no stake

The people who build your business have no ownership in its success. High turnover. No alignment. No long-term commitment.

No operational transparency

Hospitality offers no standardised reporting, no operational visibility, no alignment between the people running venues and the people who support them. The system punishes the businesses holding it up.

How It Works

One ecosystem. Three ways in.

For Venues

Certified operations. Shared infrastructure.

IX certifies your operations against 10 pillars, connects you to shared procurement, and gives your staff ownership through IXT. You keep your brand. We provide the infrastructure.

For Members

One membership. Every venue.

One membership works across every IX-certified venue. Curated entry. Founding Members get personal attention as the network grows.

For Partners

Transparency by design.

Standardised operations, verified reporting, and shared infrastructure across the network. Details on partnership available on request.
