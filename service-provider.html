Service Provider Network

Build once. Deploy across every IX venue.

Initiative X is a certified network of independent hospitality businesses. Service Providers (design, accounting, legal, HR, tech, marketing) get one ingress, one standard, one distribution engine across the whole network.

What you get

Why join as a Service Provider

Network distribution

Vetted once, deployed across every IX-certified venue. No per-client pitching. You build the template, we route the venues.

Cash + IXT revenue

Work paid in cash (your standard terms) plus IXT — the ecosystem unit (1 IXT = £1). Accept up to 50% of invoice value in IXT if it suits your cashflow.

10-Pillar certified status

Verified → Excellence tier. Quantitative, auditable, and visible across the network. Not a logo — a trust signal.

Recurring cross-referrals

Featured Supplier placements, inbound introductions from IX Ops, referral bonuses for bringing in other vetted providers.

Who this is for

Categories we work with

Any service that an independent hospitality venue would procure. Examples:

  • Design · Branding · Interior — venue build-out, signage, brand identity
  • Accounting · Bookkeeping · Tax — Xero, HMRC, VAT, year-end
  • Legal · Contracts · Compliance — lease, licensing, employment
  • HR · Payroll · Recruitment — Opsyte, hiring, L&D
  • Marketing · PR · Social — launches, campaigns, ongoing
  • Operations · Consultancy · SOPs — P&L, opening, mystery guest
  • Tech · Software · Automation — Notion, POS, CRM, n8n
  • Photography · Video · Content — venue shoots, menu, ongoing content
  • Insurance · Risk · H&S — PII, public liability, compliance audits
Note. SPs are vetted against a 10-pillar framework (adapted from our venue framework) and certified at Foundation, Verified, or Excellence tier. Certification determines directory placement, referral priority, and pricing terms.
How it works

From application to live listing

1

Submit intake

5-minute form. Company basics, service category, pricing model. Optional evidence (contracts, insurance) collected at Certification stage.

2

Desktop review (3 business days)

IX Ops reviews your submission. Portfolio check, references check, documentation review.

3

30-minute call

Video call to verify fit, discuss first venue match, agree on tier (Foundation / Verified / Excellence).

4

Agreement executed

Service Provider Network Agreement v2.0 signed via DocuSign. Onboarded to IX directory.

5

First venue introduction

Live listing in IX directory. First referral facilitated by IX Ops. Starts earning IXT + cash revenue.

Governance

Documents & terms

Before submitting, it's worth knowing what you'll be signing up to:

  • Service Provider Network Agreement v2.0 — the framework contract. Standard UK English law, 90-day termination, no exclusivity.
  • IX 10-Pillar Framework (SP-adapted) — how you're scored.
  • IXT Utility Framework v2.0 — how IXT works (1 IXT = £1 fixed, ecosystem-only, earn through delivery).

These are shared with you before the 30-minute call. No agreement is signed until you've had a chance to read and discuss.
