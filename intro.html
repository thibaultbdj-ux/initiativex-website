The thing that makes this work as a single system rather than four separate products is IXT — the loyalty currency that flows between all four parties.

IXT — the connecting tissue

IXT is a regulated loyalty unit. Not crypto. Not a tradeable token. A fund-wrapped unit with a £1 floor (the 1:1 Rule) that members earn at venues and spend across the network.

It exists so that value created in one corner of the ecosystem can flow to another without leaking out as cash discount or breakage. Members earn it spending at venues. Venues use it to attract members and pay for services. Brands fund it as activation budget. Investors hold fund units denominated in it. Staff at venues earn annual grants in it via an Employee Benefit Trust.

1 IXT = £1 floor Earned, not bought Spendable in-network FCA path: CP25/28

02How the layers fit together

Five layers. You enter through whichever one fits your role. The layers compound — each one adds value to the others.

Platform
The OS itself: governance, partnerships, infrastructure, member portal, dashboards.
Membership
Two-tier consumer model. Explorer (free) for discovery; Patron (£30/mo) for full benefits, IXT earning, events, investment access.
Certification
10-pillar venue assessment (Operations, Sustainability, Financial Health, People & HR, Customer Experience, Supply Chain, Governance, Community, Growth, Economic Participation). Three tiers: Foundation (40%+), Verified (60%+), Excellence (80%+). Also drives R-rating (R5 pre-opening → R1 mature) which feeds the fund valuation.
Fund
OEIC / Unit Trust under FCA CP25/28. Holds Business NFTs of certified venues. NAV-tracked. Six investment vehicles under an appointed FCA-authorised fund manager. Stage 0 founding preferential return, 5-stage waterfall.
White-label / Z partner
Brands, hotel groups, family offices build on IX infrastructure under their own front door. Founding Z economics: 1/2/3% management fee escalator across Y1–Y3. Vinicon (wine merchant) is the first signed Z partner.
