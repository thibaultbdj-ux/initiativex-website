The Founding 100 is the first cohort of people building Initiative X. Five segments · one hundred seats · invite-only.

You're getting this because someone (probably me) thinks you'd be one of them. This page tells you what the cohort is, what you get, and where the build actually is. If it lands, the form at the bottom is how you say so. If it doesn't, no follow-up.

01What this is

Initiative X is a shared operating system for independent hospitality. Five layers: platform, membership, certification, fund, white-label. UKIYO London is the first certified venue. Vinicon is the first certified supplier. We're picking the next 98 over the next 6–12 months.

The Founding 100 is split across five archetypes: Z partners (brands, hotel groups, family offices building on IX infrastructure), Patron-investors (capital markets pros backing the fund), operators (independent venues joining the network), service providers (the back-of-house layer), and consumer Patrons (the demand engine). Each archetype gets different mechanics. Pick yours below or describe it in the form.

For the structural detail of how the platform works (vehicles, waterfall, methodology, regulatory posture), see /overview. This page is a quicker read about what you get for being early.
