05What "your founding Z position" would look like inside IX

Same template applies for any of the four remaining founding Z slots:

  1. Initial conversation. 30-min walkthrough of the Brand Supplier Value Props (sales-enablement brief) and the founding Z escalator economics. No sales pitch — structural fit assessment.
  2. Term sheet. Founding-Z-specific terms (1/2/3% escalator, £15K setup waived, first refusal on rounds, founding buy-in into the Ecosystem Fund).
  3. Onboarding. 4–6 weeks to live. Brand portfolio integration, member-facing surface configuration, white-label network coverage.
  4. Activation begins. Patron-only events, trade education, allocation drops. Activation budget paid in IXT — compounds in the network rather than breaking as discount.
  5. Quarterly review. Member spend data, activation ROI, venue coverage, IXT-in-Fund-NFT position. First-refusal pipeline visible.
