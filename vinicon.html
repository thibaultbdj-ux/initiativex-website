Common Questions
What do I get as a Patron? +
Patron membership is £30/month and unlocks:
  • 10% preferential pricing on every purchase across the network — the saving is converted into IXT for you (the main Patron benefit)
  • 1.10% IXT earn rate on spend (vs 1% for Explorers)
  • Access to Chez Vous — the digital wine cellar, pre-orders at cost price, storage, and preferential corkage at partner restaurants
  • Flash codes — time-limited boosted returns, delivered in-app
  • Pre-opening access to new partner venues and Vinicon events
  • Investment platform access (required for fund participation)
  • Activity-based IXT earning (referrals, reviews, milestones)
  • Ability to spend IXT on products and services across the ecosystem
How does Chez Vous work? +
Pre-order premium bottles through Vinicon at cost-plus-margin (not restaurant markup). Wine is held in bonded storage in your digital cellar. Bring any bottle to a partner restaurant — you pay a corkage fee instead of wine list prices. Available to Patron members.
What is IXT? +
IXT is the IX loyalty currency. 1 IXT = GBP 1 (fixed face value, always). Explorer members earn 1% of spend in IXT. Patron members earn 1.10% of spend plus 10% preferential pricing (converted to IXT) on every purchase. Redeemable across all IX partner venues and merchants.
How do I cancel or change my tier? +
You can downgrade or cancel at any time from your account settings. IXT balance carries over. Patron allocations revert to Explorer-level access. No lock-in, no exit fees.
Who stores my wine? +
Wine is held in bonded storage managed by Vinicon. Full provenance tracking, insurance, and temperature-controlled conditions. You can request delivery or reserve for Chez Vous at any time.
