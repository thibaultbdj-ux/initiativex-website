01What being IX-certified actually means for this venue

UKIYO sits inside IX as the reference example for the Foundation tier. Concretely:

  • Member traffic. IX Patrons see UKIYO in the curated venue map; bookings flow through the IX concierge layer where they choose to use it.
  • Loyalty engine. Patron spend at UKIYO earns IXT at the Master v6.1 rate (1.10% of spend). IXT can be redeemed at UKIYO or at any other IX-certified venue.
  • Quarterly assessment. The 10-pillar score is reassessed every quarter. Improvement → tier-up to Verified (60%+) or Excellence (80%+) → lower Governance Premium in the WACC build → higher assessed Business NFT value.
  • Capital access. Once the IX fund manager is authorised, UKIYO becomes eligible for Cashflow Notes (working capital) and Development Convertibles (refit / expansion). Stage 0 founding preferential return applies on any venue-specific raise.
  • Brand activation surface. Z-partner brands (Vinicon today; more to come) can run member tastings, allocation drops and pairing series at UKIYO. Activation budgets paid in IXT — loops back into the venue.

05What "your venue" would look like inside IX

Same template applies for any venue joining IX Phase 1:

  1. Apply for certification. 10-pillar self-assessment with evidence (financials, lease, H&S, supplier list). Reviewed by IX assessment team. ~5 business days for completeness review, ~15 for full assessment.
  2. Tier awarded. Foundation (40%+), Verified (60%+), or Excellence (80%+). Score by pillar visible to the operator and to IX.
  3. Live in the member-facing layer. Patron members see the venue. IXT mechanics activate.
  4. Quarterly reassessment. Operator can improve specific pillars to lift tier → lift R-rating → lower WACC Governance Premium → better fund-side terms.
  5. Capital + brand surface activates as the regulatory + fund-manager rails come live.
