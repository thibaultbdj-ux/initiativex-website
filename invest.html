Methodology

How valuations work

All Business NFT valuations follow the IX Business NFT Valuation Methodology v2.0 — a stage-dependent DCF framework with:

  • Four-layer WACC — Base Cost of Capital + Stage Premium (R1–R5) + Certification Premium (Foundation/Verified/Excellence) + Governance Premium (G1–G5)
  • Capex floor protecting against downside — venue valued at minimum its real added-value-to-property
  • Debt deducted at every stage — Enterprise Value minus Total Financial Debt = Equity Value
  • Lease renewal probability weighting on terminal value
  • Reported numbers only — no normalisation below R2 stage
  • Quarterly review by IX Finance, with independent valuer sign-off at stage upgrades

Questions

Before you register

Some questions worth thinking through:

  • Are you registering for yourself (retail), on behalf of a family office, or as an institutional investor?
  • What capacity per deal are you comfortable with (£5K / £25K / £100K / £500K+)?
  • What's your horizon (12 months, 3 years, 7+ years)?
  • Which vehicles interest you most (short-duration notes, long-term fund equity, venue-specific NFTs)?
  • Do you meet the FCA self-certification criteria (HNW: £100K+ income or £250K+ net assets ex-residence; Sophisticated: 2+ years in finance or 3+ unlisted investments in last 2 years)?

None of these are required to register — they're covered in the form — but thinking through them helps.
